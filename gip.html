Al Ginn

Four kids are outside fighting over who gets to be a seeker as they play hide and seek. Once they all have decided they go their separate ways, and now we are focused on this little boy Ali who gets distracted by a beautiful butterfly. The butterfly leads him to this beautiful house… or so it seems. Ali, with no hesitation, enters the house out of childish curiosity. Inside, it’s dusty and creepy, not as nice, clean, or bright as he thought.

As he walks in, he steps on a funny-looking doll and picks it up. The doll starts to slowly turn its head and talk… “Welcome ya Ali." After the doll says this, the door slams shut with a powerful force, and the mansion becomes freezing cold. Ali manages to escape in time and tells his friends what happened. Scared, they all run away from the area.

THE END.
